# What Stays Human

- Customer-impacting migration approvals.
- Data retention and privacy policy decisions.
- Identity merge conflict policy.
- Budget-vs-latency tradeoffs.
- Legal interpretation of GDPR/CCPA erasure requirements.
- Final go/no-go during an incident or launch spike.
- Whether to adopt non-breaking SDK telemetry changes.
- Commercial decision to isolate a noisy tenant or charge for dedicated capacity.
